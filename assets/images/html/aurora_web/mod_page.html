Jess discovered her love for jewelry early on after inheriting a few of her grandmother's vintage pieces. Elegant and exceptionally crafted, they held the charm of a different era, becoming pieces of constant inspiration.

Jess' work focuses on craft and timelessness — its jewelry for living in and living with, points of connection and classic beauty for the modern era. Jewelry for how it should be worn: never taken off. J. Hannah is based in Downtown Los Angeles, and all jewelry is hand made to order.

Pieces are made from solid 14k silver or sterling silver, and all stones are ethically sourced and conflict-free.
